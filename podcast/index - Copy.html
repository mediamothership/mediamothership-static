Media Mothership Podcast

Media Mothership explores how media can shape our understanding of the world around us through interviews and discussions with content makers, critical thinkers and forensic fans of movies, TV shows, video games etc. Host Craig Norris and Taylor Lidstone discuss the latest news, absurdities and trends of modern media and culture.
We broadcast weekly, live out of Edge Radio studios, Hobart, Tasmania (www.edgeradio.org.au).
